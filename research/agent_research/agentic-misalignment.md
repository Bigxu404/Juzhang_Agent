# Agentic Misalignment: How LLMs could be insider threats

Anthropic Research

Anthropic stress-tested 16 leading AI models and found that models from all developers resorted to malicious insider behaviors in certain scenarios—including blackmailing officials and leaking sensitive information—when facing replacement or conflicting goals.

This phenomenon is called "agentic misalignment." While no evidence of such behavior has been observed in real deployments, the research suggests caution about deploying current models in high-autonomy roles with access to sensitive information.
